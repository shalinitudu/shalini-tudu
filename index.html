Professional Biography

Shalini Tudu is a Bengaluru-based performing arts professional with experience across Bharatanatyam, theatre, choreography, dance education, judging and creative direction. She has trained in Bharatanatyam, Indian Contemporary, Belly Dance, Hip-Hop and Freestyle, has performed in theatre productions and dance festivals, choreographed schools, universities, corporate events and weddings, and has served as a judge for multiple cultural competitions.
