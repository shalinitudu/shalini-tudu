Professional Profile

A multidisciplinary performing artist

Shalini Tudu is a Bengaluru-based actor, theatre artist, choreographer, trained Bharatanatyam dancer, performing arts educator and judge with over twelve years of experience across dance, theatre, choreography and stage productions.

Her multidisciplinary experience spans acting, theatrical performance, classical and contemporary dance, creative direction, choreography, performance mentoring and performance evaluation.
